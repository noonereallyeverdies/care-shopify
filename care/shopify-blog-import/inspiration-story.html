A Journey of Love and Resilience: The care•atin Story

I never planned to create a hair care device. But sometimes life changes your path in unexpected ways.

When my aunt Lisa was diagnosed with breast cancer at 42, I watched as treatments took not just her health, but also her beautiful hair. Even after beating cancer, her hair grew back thinner and more brittle than before.

"It's just hair," she would say with a smile. But I could see how much it affected her confidence.

Around the same time, I began my own struggle with hair thinning due to hormonal changes. Expensive serums, supplements, and treatments gave little results. It felt like nothing worked.

The Science Discovery

That's when I discovered red light therapy during my biomedical engineering studies. The science fascinated me – how specific light wavelengths could stimulate cells and promote healing without side effects.

The research was compelling:

  • Red light (650-670nm wavelength) can penetrate the scalp to reach hair follicles
  • The light energizes mitochondria in cells, helping produce more ATP (cellular energy)
  • This boost can extend the active growth phase of hair follicles
  • Studies showed improved hair density, thickness, and reduced shedding

From Prototype to Purpose

I built the first care•atin prototype in my apartment, testing it on myself and my aunt. After three months, we both saw real results – new growth, less shedding, and stronger hair.

What began as a personal project became my mission: to create an effective, non-invasive solution for women facing hair challenges. Something that truly works, designed by someone who understands the emotional journey.

"When I started seeing new baby hairs along my hairline, I cried. It wasn't just about looking better – it was about feeling like myself again." – Lisa, breast cancer survivor
